## Design principles

When turning this outline into slides, keep the deck:
- Clear and visually simple.
- Focused on one main idea per slide.
- Light on text.
- Strong on modelling and worked examples.
- Easy for students to follow.
- Easy for the teacher to present from.

## Slide planning checklist

- [ ] The lesson purpose is clear.
- [ ] Each slide has one main focus.
- [ ] The deck moves logically from explanation to practice.
- [ ] The lesson includes modelling.
- [ ] The lesson includes student practice.
- [ ] The lesson ends with review or reflection.
- [ ] The slides support, rather than replace, teacher explanation.

## Notes

This file is intended to be a planning document rather than a finished presentation. A strong outline should make the final slide deck faster to build and easier to improve later.
